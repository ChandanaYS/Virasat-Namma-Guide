Smart Heritage Tourism App

Virasat-Namma Guide

A Kotlin and Jetpack Compose Android app that helps visitors discover Karnataka's heritage places, scan QR codes for hidden facts, listen to audio history, and collect check-ins like a digital travel passport.

View GitHub Repository Download APK

Project Overview

Built For Meaningful Heritage Exploration

Virasat-Namma Guide brings together location-based discovery, cultural storytelling, QR scanning, audio narration, and travel history tracking in one clean mobile experience. The app is designed for tourists, students, and heritage enthusiasts who want a simple way to explore historical places with context and confidence.

Core Features

Everything Needed For A Smart Tourism App

📍

Location Discovery

Explore nearby Karnataka heritage places with radius filters and clean place cards.

📷

QR Scanner

Scan heritage QR codes using CameraX and Google ML Kit to open the correct place page.

🔊

Audio Guide

Listen to history narration through an optimized Android text-to-speech audio guide.

✅

Travel Passport

Check in at places and save visited heritage locations using Room Database.

QR Scanner Feature

The app scans custom QR payloads such as virasat://place/hampi. After scanning, it opens the matching heritage page and reveals a hidden fact.

Audio Guide Feature

The audio guide reads place history aloud with managed audio focus, clean playback, and smooth narration behavior for a better tourism experience.

Travel Passport Feature

Users can check in at visited places and view their travel history, creating a simple digital memory of heritage exploration.
